Nike company


(marketing)
Nike, Inc. is a multinational corporation based in Beaverton, Oregon, and is one of the world's largest suppliers of athletic shoes, apparel, and sports equipment. Founded in 1964 as Blue Ribbon Sports by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, the company was rebranded as Nike in 1971, named after the Greek goddess of victory. Known for its innovative designs, marketing strategies, and high-quality products, Nike has become a symbol of athletic excellence and success. The iconic "swoosh" logo and the "Just Do It" slogan are recognized worldwide, and Nike's endorsement deals with top athletes further cement its reputation as a leader in the sports industry.
